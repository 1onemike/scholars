About Scholars International

Scholars' International School Family has experienced educators. They distinguish themselves by their passion, to educate and their love for children. They firmly believe and understand the importance of pre-education that prepares the young ones to step the world of schooling for the next 12-15 years of their growing year. The school ensures a low child-teacher ratio for each play classroom to ensure adequate attention to each child. While recognizing the importance of group play in the learning process, the our-riding objective is to ensure each child enjoys while getting moulded into an individual with a strong sense of self-worth, values, integrity and self-esteem.
